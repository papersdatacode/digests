*Yihao Wang, Yuheng Ji, Mingyu Cao et al.*

It learns one next-state latent from video and language, then probes frozen backbones for text, images, and robot actions.

*Shen Nie, Qiyang Min, Shaoxuan Xu et al.*

Keeps masked diffusion end-to-end at 8B scale, matching Qwen2.5-7B and surpassing LLaDA by 14.5-21.6 points on reasoning benchmarks.

[→ Full breakdown](https://t.me/papersdatacode/229)

*Lei Bai, Zongsheng Cao, Yang Chen et al.*

It matches 1T-class agent results with 35B parameters by scaling 45K-token horizons and merging six specialists via domain-routed on-policy distillation.
